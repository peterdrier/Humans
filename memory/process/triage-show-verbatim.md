When triaging feedback reports, always show the reporter's full verbatim description in the presentation to Peter — not just a one-line summary. The summary + analysis reflect interpretation, which can be wrong, and Peter needs the ground truth to steer the decision.

**Why:** Peter pointed out that a one-line summary of a Question/Bug report hid context he needed to decide what to do. The reporter's full words often contain nuance (tone, specifics, emotional register) the summary can't capture.

**How to apply:** In the feedback triage table, include a column (or a quoted block per report) with the reporter's exact `Description` text, verbatim. Keep the analysis/proposed-action columns alongside, but don't substitute summary for the original text.
