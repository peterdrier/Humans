## Question

How do I join a team? I don't see a Join button on the Kitchen team page.

## Answer

To join a team, go to the team's detail page and look for the join/request option there. A couple of things that could explain a missing Join button:

1. **You may already have a pending request** — if you previously requested to join, the button is replaced by a "Withdraw request" option.
2. **You may already be a member** — members don't see a Join button.
3. **The team requires coordinator approval** — the button should still appear, but it submits a join *request* rather than adding you immediately. If it's genuinely missing, the team's page may be showing limited information.

**Next steps:**
- Double-check the Kitchen team's detail page (not the public directory listing).
- If the button is still absent and none of the above apply, use the feedback widget to report it and a coordinator can look into it.
