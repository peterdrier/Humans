# Joining a Team

Great question! Here's how to join:

## Standard join flow

1. Go to the **Teams** section and browse or search for the team you want to join.
2. Open the team's detail page.
3. Click the **Join** or **Request to Join** button (depending on whether the team requires coordinator approval).

## Why you might not see a Join button

The most common reasons:

- **You're already a member** — check your profile under "My Teams" to confirm.
- **The team requires approval** — the button may say "Request to Join" instead. You'll submit a request and wait for a coordinator to approve it.
- **The team is hidden** — some teams are only visible to coordinators and admins.

## For the Kitchen team specifically

If you still don't see a button on the Kitchen team page, it's likely one of the scenarios above. You can:

- Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.
- Check if you're already listed as a member on the team's roster.
- Reach out to the Kitchen team's coordinator directly — they can add you manually if needed.
