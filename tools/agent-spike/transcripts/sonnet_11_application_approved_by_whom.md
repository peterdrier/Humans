## Question

I submitted a Colaborador application. Who approves it and how long does it take?

## Answer

Your Colaborador application goes through a **Board vote and decision** process:

1. **Board members cast individual votes** on your application.
2. **Board or Admin approves or rejects it**, recording a decision note and the Board meeting date.

There's no documented SLA for how long this takes — it depends on when the Board meets. If you want to follow up on timing, I'd suggest using the **feedback widget** to reach a coordinator who can check the queue.

Once approved, your tier updates automatically and you're added to the Colaboradors team. Your term runs for 2 years, expiring on **31 December of the next odd year** at least 2 years from approval.

You can track your application status under your own governance view in the app.
