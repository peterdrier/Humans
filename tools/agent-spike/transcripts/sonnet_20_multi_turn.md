## Question

Walk me through what I have to do next, step by step, so I can actually start volunteering.

## Answer

Here's where you are and what's left:

## Your onboarding status

You're authenticated and your profile exists, but two things need to happen (in any order) before you're activated as a Volunteer:

---

## Step 1 — Sign your pending documents

You have two documents waiting for your consent:

- **Privacy Policy**
- **Member Contract**

Go to the **Legal / Consent** section and sign both. Once the last one is signed, your consent check status will move to **Pending** and the coordinator review queue will be notified.

---

## Step 2 — Pass the profile review

A **Consent Coordinator** needs to review and clear your profile. This happens independently of the documents — they may clear it before or after you finish signing. You don't need to do anything to trigger it; it's on the coordinator's queue.

---

## What happens when both are done

Once your documents are signed **and** your profile review is cleared, you're automatically added to the Volunteers team and a welcome email is sent. No extra action needed from you.

---

## While you wait

You currently have access to: your profile, the consent/legal pages, public team pages, and the feedback widget. Most of the app (shifts, teams management, governance, etc.) unlocks once you're fully activated.

If you think there's a delay on the coordinator review, you can flag it using the **feedback widget**.
