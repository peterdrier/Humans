# Leave the Kitchen Team

You can leave the Kitchen team directly from your profile:

1. Go to **Profile > My Teams** (or navigate to your profile and select the Teams section)
2. Find **Kitchen** in your list of teams
3. Click **Leave** or the remove button next to it
4. Confirm the action

Once you leave, you'll no longer see Kitchen shifts, announcements, or member roster.

**Note:** If you're a manager or have a special role on the team, leaving will remove that role assignment as well.

If you run into any issues, use the feedback widget to let us know.
