## Question

I joined the Build team but I don't see the Build shared drive in my Google Drive. What's wrong?

## Answer

Hi Marta! Great question. Google Drive folder access is provisioned automatically when you're added to a team, but there are a couple of things to check:

**First, make sure you're using the right Google account:**
- The system syncs Drive access using your registered Google service email (the one linked to your Nobodies Collective account). If you have multiple Google accounts, make sure you're logged into the one connected here.

**If you're on the right account, the sync may still be in progress:**
- Google resource sync runs periodically, so there can be a short delay (usually a few minutes) between joining and the folder appearing. Try refreshing your Drive in a few minutes.

**If it's been longer than that**, there may be a technical issue on the sync side. I don't have visibility into sync logs from here, but I can route this to a coordinator or admin who can investigate.

**In the meantime**, you can ask a Build team coordinator to verify you're listed as a member in the team's roster — if you're not showing up there, that's the first thing to fix.
