## Question

I submitted a Colaborador application. Who approves it and how long does it take?

## Answer

# Colaborador Application Review

Your application goes to the **Board** for a vote. Here's how it works:

## The Process

1. **Board members vote individually** on your application — each Board member casts one vote.
2. **Board decides** (approve, reject, or ask for more info) and records a collective decision note and meeting date.
3. **You're notified** of the outcome by email.

## Timeline

The documentation doesn't specify a standard review window — it depends on the Board's meeting schedule. I'd recommend:

- **Check your email** for updates from the system.
- **Ask your coordinator or a Board member directly** if you want to know the next Board meeting date.

## What Happens If Approved

If approved, you'll:
- Be added to the **Colaboradors system team**
- Get a **2-year term** expiring on December 31 of an odd year (e.g., 2027, 2029)
- Receive a welcome notification
