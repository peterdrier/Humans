## Question

How do I leave the Kitchen team?

## Answer

On any team's detail page there's a **Leave** button. Here's how to get there:

1. Go to **Teams** in the nav.
2. Find **Kitchen** and open its detail page.
3. Click **Leave team**.

Your membership is removed immediately. Note that leaving a team also removes any role assignments you hold on that team.

If you don't see a Leave option, it may be a system team — but Kitchen isn't one, so you should be good to go.
