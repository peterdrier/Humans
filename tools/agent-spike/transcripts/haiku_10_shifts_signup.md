## Question

How do I sign up for shifts? I can see the Shifts page but nothing seems clickable.

## Answer

Hey Nico! Shift signup availability depends on a couple of things:

1. **Shift browsing needs to be open.** Your coordinator controls whether regular volunteers can browse shifts. If browsing is closed, you can only see shifts if you already have signups on them.

2. **You need existing signups or browsing permission.** Once browsing is enabled, you'll be able to click on shifts to sign up.

Check with your department coordinator (likely someone on the Build team) to confirm that shift browsing is open for the current event. If it should be open and still isn't clickable, let us know via the feedback widget and we can dig into it.
